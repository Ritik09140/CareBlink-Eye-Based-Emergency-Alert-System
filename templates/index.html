How CareBlink Eye Tracking Works

1
Eye Detection

Webcam records video. MediaPipe Face Mesh localizes eye contours (6 key facial landmarks per eye) in real time.

2
Calculate EAR

Computer Vision computes the Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR). When eyes close, EAR drops significantly below 0.22.

3
Count Blinks

A closed-to-open transition increases the blink count. 5 blinks in ≤ 5 seconds initiates the API dispatch.

4
Dashboard Alert

Flask handles the request, inserts it into MySQL, and broadcasts the alert. Browser plays buzzer beep synthetically.
